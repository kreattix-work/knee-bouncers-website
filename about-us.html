Play-based learning with purpose

KneeBouncers’ Educational Games

KneeBouncers develops engaging, play-based games designed to stimulate children’s curiosity, creativity, and learning in key areas including mathematics, science, language, literacy, social and emotional development, as well as arts and music. Our approach supports concrete learning objectives within an age-appropriate framework that grows with the child, ensuring our games remain relevant and challenging from infancy through PreK. This play-with-purpose strategy ensures each game not only entertains but also educates, promoting developmental growth at every stage.

Game development

The logic model for KneeBouncer games starts with engaging the child through interactive and fun inputs, leading to educational activities where learning objectives are met with playful ease. Feedback is integral, ensuring that children understand their actions’ outcomes, fostering a positive learning environment. This cycle promotes growth in key developmental areas, achieved through a structured yet flexible approach to increasing complexity and skill-building.

This generalized logic model for KneeBouncer games emphasizes several key components: engagement, education, feedback, and growth
