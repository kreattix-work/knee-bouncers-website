Play-based learning with purpose

KneeBouncers’ Educational Games

KneeBouncers develops engaging, play-based games designed to stimulate children’s curiosity, creativity, and learning in key areas including mathematics, science, language, literacy, social and emotional development, as well as arts and music. Our approach supports concrete learning objectives within an age-appropriate framework that grows with the child, ensuring our games remain relevant and challenging from infancy through PreK. This play-with-purpose strategy ensures each game not only entertains but also educates, promoting developmental growth at every stage.

Game development

The logic model for KneeBouncer games starts with engaging the child through interactive and fun inputs, leading to educational activities where learning objectives are met with playful ease. Feedback is integral, ensuring that children understand their actions’ outcomes, fostering a positive learning environment. This cycle promotes growth in key developmental areas, achieved through a structured yet flexible approach to increasing complexity and skill-building.

This generalized logic model for KneeBouncer games emphasizes several key components: engagement, education, feedback, and growth

Universal pre-kindergarten
learning objectives

While KneeBouncers' games are not aligned with a specific formal curriculum, they are developed in consultation with early childhood education experts to ensure that they support key developmental milestones and learning standards appropriate for toddlers and preschoolers. The games are structured around universal pre-kindergarten learning objectives that focus on the holistic development of the child, including:

Cognitive Development: Enhancing memory, problem-solving skills, and the ability to recognize patterns and categorize objects.

Language Development: Expanding vocabulary, understanding basic phonics, and encouraging language use through interactive gameplay.

Fine Motor Skills: Improving coordination and control through tapping, dragging, and other touch-screen interactions.

Social-Emotional Growth: Fostering patience, attention, and the joy of learning through positive reinforcement and engaging feedback mechanisms.

KneeBouncers’ games are designed to grow with our young learners, offering a range of complexities to match their developing skills. Our approach includes:

Early Learning: Enhancing memory, problem-solving skills, and the ability to recognize patterns and categorize objects.

Advancing Skills: Expanding vocabulary, understanding basic phonics, and encouraging language use through interactive gameplay.

Adaptive Complexity: Improving coordination and control through tapping, dragging, and other touch-screen interactions.
