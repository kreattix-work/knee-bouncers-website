About our videos

Our videos teach young children about the world around them. From the BabyFirst favorite “The KneeBouncers’s Show” series, which introduces learning concepts in a funny short videos, to “Kiki’s Music Time” with classic singalong songs, nursery rhymes, and original songs. Your child will love all the singing, dancing, learning, and fun!

Educational Fun with The KneeBouncers Show

Let your child discover the magic of play with “The KneeBouncers’ Show”! Our funny and engaging educational videos teach children about shapes, colors, animals, and more through charming stories. Perfect for all ages, they’re an excellent way to introduce new concepts in an entertaining way

Learning Through Song with Kiki’s Music Time

Get your kids grooving with Kiki’s Music Time! Sing along to classic kids’ songs and nursery rhymes and learn about colors, shapes, and animals through these energetic videos. They offer not only loads of fun but also a wealth of educational content, keeping kids delighted as they learn.

Our Commitment to Age-Appropriate Learning

At KneeBouncers, we’re dedicated to creating age-appropriate content that enriches the lives of young children. Working with early education experts, we ensure our content has a positive impact, providing entertaining and meaningful learning experiences.
